Delete Your Account

You can delete your Lumi account and all associated data at any time — directly in the app or by contacting us. This page explains how, and what happens to your data when you do.

How to delete your account

The easiest way is directly inside the Lumi app:

  1. Open the Lumi app
  2. Tap your profile icon to go to Settings
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Can't access the app? Email us at hello@getlumi.today with the subject "Account deletion request" and we will manually delete your account within 7 days.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your user account and login credentials
  • All children's profiles associated with your account
  • All uploaded drawings and source images
  • All generated images and animations in your gallery
  • Any partner account links
  • Your remaining credit balance

What is retained

In limited cases, certain data may be retained beyond account deletion where required by law:

Data type Retention period Reason
Transaction records 10 years Statutory accounting obligation (§ 147 AO)
Anonymised usage data Up to 12 months Aggregate analytics only — not linked to your identity
