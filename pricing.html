Questions

How do I activate my subscription in the app?
Use the same email address when subscribing here and when signing up in the app. Your premium status activates automatically within a few seconds of payment.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel directly in your Stripe billing portal — your subscription remains active until the end of the paid period.
What happens when I hit my monthly animation limit?
You can purchase additional animation credits at any time from within the app. They never expire.
Why subscribe here instead of in the app?
Subscribing on the website saves you ~15% because we avoid the App Store fee — and we pass that saving on to you.
Is my payment secure?
All payments are processed by Stripe — the same payment infrastructure used by Airbnb, Spotify, and millions of other businesses. We never see your card details.
Can I change my subscription?
Yes. You can upgrade from a monthly to an annual plan at any time — Stripe will automatically prorate the remaining days and apply the credit. Downgrades from annual to monthly are not available, but you can cancel at the end of your billing period.
