Common questions

How do I get started with Lumi?
Download Lumi, create an account, add a child profile, and take a photo of any drawing. Choose a style and watch the magic happen.
What is a credit?
Each animation uses one credit. Credits are added to your account monthly based on your plan. Unused credits carry over.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings in the app, or by visiting your subscription management page.
Can I share my subscription with my partner?
Yes — Lumi Family includes partner sharing. You can invite your partner from the Settings screen and they'll get access to the shared credit pool and all children's galleries.
My animation is taking a long time. Is that normal?
Animations typically take 30–90 seconds to generate. During busy periods it may take a little longer. You'll be notified when it's ready.
How do I save or share an animation?
Open any creation in the gallery and tap the save or share button. You can save directly to your Photos or share via any app on your device.
I have a question not listed here.
