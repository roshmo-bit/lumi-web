How Lumi works

Three steps to a
perfect animation

From crayon drawing to animated video — it really is this simple.

01

Photograph the drawing

Open Lumi, select a child's profile and take a photo of the drawing — or upload an existing image from your gallery.

02

Choose a style

Pick from four magical art styles: Photorealistic, Storybook, Anime or Cinematic. More styles coming soon.

03

Reveal & be amazed

Gather the family around the screen and tap "Reveal" together — and experience the magical moment.
